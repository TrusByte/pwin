Rules and Reward Logic
01. Participation Fee
To participate in the jackpot, a fee of

0.00 POL

is required. This amount is added to the reward pool. The participation fee is non-refundable under all circumstances. A small fee(≈ 0.1POL) is allocated to the contract creator for maintaining Chainlink VRF and covering operational expenses.
02. Random Number Generation
The contract generates a secure random

4-digit number

4-digit number. This process is powered by Chainlink VRF. The randomness cannot be controlled or influenced by any party, including Chainlink VRF or validators.
03. Winning Conditions
The number generated by Chainlink VRF must meet the following conditions to be eligible for a win: The number must end with the digit 3. The number must be a prime number. Both conditions must be satisfied to qualify for a win or to be assigned as the Prime Address.
04. Prime Address Logic
Initially, there is no Prime Address (set to zero) If the Prime Address is empty and a user meets the winning condition, they become the new Prime Address Only one Prime Address exists at a time When a winner appears and Prime Address is set, rewards are distributed among winner and Prime Address After reward distribution, Prime Address resets back to zero
05. Reward Distribution
When the winning condition is met and a Prime Address exists, a jackpot is triggered. The reward is distributed between the winner and the Prime Address.

70%

of the reward is allocated to the winner.

30%

of the reward is allocated to the Prime Address.
06. Decentralization & Fairness
No admin or external party can control the outcome All logic is executed automatically by the smart contract Randomness is provided securely by Chainlink VRF No one can manipulate or predict results
07. Continuous Process
Each new user repeats the same process The reward pool keeps increasing with every entry The system runs continuously without interruption
